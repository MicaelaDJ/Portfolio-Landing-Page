Why I took a programming course at Epicodus

I decided to learn programming for a number of reasons. It had always been in the back of my mind as an interesting skill to have, but until recently the time had just never been right. It was a skill I first realized I needed when my friend and I wanted to make video games, but we were having trouble getting a programmer that would stick and had the same ideals that we did. So, since my friend was already supplying some art and design, I decided to learn programming so that besides just art I could also add more to the game we wanted to make. Another reason was that the most recent job I had was a situation that I no longer wished to be a part of. I wanted more and better career opportunities. After some research I decided that Epicodus was the best school for me. I'm not one to wait for the "right" moment, so I packed my bags, moved across the United States, and found a place where I could study and be close to the school.
